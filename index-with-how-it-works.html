What is Passana?

Passana is a way of living. It is the ability to make powerful healthy choices every day that enrich and fulfil your life.

In its simplest form, Passana is a collection of freshly pressed juices using the finest, locally sourced, organic ingredients.

Passana juices are created with specific health benefits in mind. Powerful ingredients such as turmeric, lemon and ginger are combined with spring water to deliver a freshly pressed hit of nutrition.

How it works

1. Discover

Each week, Passana sends you a list of exciting new organic juices to choose from.

2. Buy

Once you're happy with your choice, buy online we'll prepare your order for next day delivery.

3. Deliver

Your freshly squeezed juices will be packed up and ready to ship to you, for next day delivery, using our high quality courier networks.
