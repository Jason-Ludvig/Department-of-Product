Super Mario Elected Mayor of Toad Town

Super Mario wins, edging past Luigi.

World in shock as video game star elected Mayor

Super Mario, the star of the popular video games, Super Mario Bros, is the newly elected mayor of Toad Town. Supported by his compatriots Luigi, Yoshi and life-long partner Princess Daisy, Mario was the clear winner in what many anticipated might be a tight race.

Mario's policies

How the world is reacting

Bowser described the news as 'Really awesome. I would have loved to have benefitted from free education.' he said.

'At last. He's finally giving the people what they want. Humans LOVE karts.' added Princess Daisy, the long term partner of Mario and first in line to the throne.

Mario is expected to address the nation in a televised special later this evening, with a global audience of almost 1 billion expected to tune in.
